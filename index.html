Who We Are

ClashForge is an independent fan-made toolkit built by a solo developer and Clash of Clans enthusiast. This site is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Supercell in any way — it is a labour of love from one chief to another.

After several years of playing Clash of Clans, the mental math never stopped — how long will this upgrade take with a boost? How many raids do I need? Which hero should I prioritise first? ClashForge was built to answer all of those questions, for free.

Our Mission

ClashForge exists to help chiefs at every Town Hall level make smarter upgrade decisions — saving time, resources, and frustration. Every tool is completely free, no account required, no data collected.
