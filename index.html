⚔ War Weight Calculator
What is War Weight? Every building, hero and defense adds a hidden "weight" value. Supercell uses total weight to match clans fairly in wars. Higher weight = tougher opponents. Rushing defenses raises weight without the offense to match.
👑 Heroes
Heroes carry the highest weight per level. A maxed BK adds ~21,000 weight alone.
🔴 Heavy Defenses
These single buildings add the most weight. Each Eagle Artillery level adds ~3,000 weight.
